This research article was done for our course assignment for the course Computer and Information Ethics.

Abstract

Online education system is getting popular day-by-day among educational instituions and students in COVID-19 pandemic. Although the examination system is considered suitable among these instituions, students are tend to take advantages by performing unethical activities as they are not invigilated physically during exams. This research tries to find the mindset of students and thoughts towards online activities, and the degree of unethical activities perform by them through surveys.
